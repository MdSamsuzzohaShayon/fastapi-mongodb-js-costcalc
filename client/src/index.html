‍‍‍Option 4: DIY u‍‍‍sing Website Builder

Building your own website using a Website Builder is always a very affordable and user-friendly option. Most functionality is already built in and will not required a lot of developer intervention. This options is usually best for individuals who have small/medium complexity websites and want to control the process of building and editing their own site. However, it is important to keep in mind that not all website builders are created equal. Tools like Squarespace and Weebly are more restrictive and are geared toward people who want to use grids to design their sites. On the other hand, tools like PageCloud and Wix are visual editors that give you much more flexibility to move your content freely on the page.
